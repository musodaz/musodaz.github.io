Food Systems Policy Tool

A Food Systems Policy Tool I developed to support the delivery of national nutrition strategies. Primarily aimed at low andmiddle income countries, the Tool will help executives and public officials take the ‘next step’ in validating diet and nutrtion-related programmes and support their implementation.

Diet quality and food systems compendium

An interactive information compendium for food systems and diets based on the work of Glopan. It is intended to introduce the reader to the concept of food systems, diets and their interconnectivity. Please note, this is a work in progress so the only working example is on biofortification.
